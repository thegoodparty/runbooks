Rules for finding and presenting budget impact for each priority item.

## What to include

- Total cost (one-time and/or recurring)
- Per-household translation at the local levy level
- Stacked impact when multiple items in the same meeting affect the same taxpayer

## Household-level translation

Always translate dollar figures to a per-household annual impact. Use the jurisdiction's total household count from Census data.

Example:
$1.2M one-time install + $180K/yr ops. Spread across the levy, that's ~$8.40/household one-time and ~$1.30/household/yr ongoing.

## Anchoring

Compare to a prior council decision when possible. This grounds the number in something familiar to the official and their constituents.

Example:
$11/household over 10 years is half what we spent on the downtown banner program.

## Numeric precision

Dollar amounts and vote counts must be extracted from source exactly -- do not round, paraphrase, or infer. If discrepancies appear between figures in different source documents, flag them rather than resolving silently.
